Attaching the String

 

1.Once you’ve attached all the dowels, you’re ready to move onto attaching the fly line.

2.Just as with the diamond kite, you’ll want to make sure your kite has the sail facing up and the frame facing down.  

3.You’ll again want to tie a bridle to your kite with string like you did for the diamond kite.  

4.Cut a piece of string about twice the length of your horizontal dowel.

5.Tie each end of the string to the ends of the horizontal dowel, and secure them in place with a little bit of tape.  

6.When you’re ready to fly, tie your fly line to the center point of the bridle string.
